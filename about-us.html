Who are we ?

Parfumerie-en-ligne.com is an online sales site for Perfumes, Care and Cosmetics.
Perfumery professionals in France for 60 years, we distribute the biggest brands of selective perfumery, with their distribution agreements.

little history...

A family business created in 1956, Parfumerie Mireille opens in Sorgues, near Avignon. Over the next few decades, 4 new points of sale will open, all in the Vaucluse department. 1st independent perfumery to have joined the PASSION BEAUTE group, its objective is to provide its customers with preferential rates, gifts and entertainment. With a view to sharing the passion of our profession with as many people as possible, this is how Parfumerie-en-ligne.com was born in 2009.
